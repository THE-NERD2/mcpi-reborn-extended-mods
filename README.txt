A collection of mods for MCPI++
Mods may or may not work on regular Minecraft Pi Reborn. They have not been tested on that game, but they should work.

All code licensed under CC0

Known problems:
- Touching any tiles created by the redstone mod causes the game to segfault
- Placing a redstone torch next to a redstone tile also causes the game to segfault
- Moving a redstone tile by means of pistons is segfaulty (hit or miss)
